About RBP FINIVIS

RBP FINIVIS is a next-generation financial services platform focused on simplifying foreign exchange and cross-border payments through technology, compliance, and automation.

We are an RBI-licensed Full-Fledged Money Changer (FFMC), delivering seamless digital access to international remittances, currency exchange, forex cards, travel insurance, and education loan facilitation all designed to serve individuals, students, travelers, and businesses moving money globally.

Our mission is simple:
make international financial services faster, safer, and easier to access.

Who We Are

RBP FINIVIS Private Limited is a regulated Indian fintech company headquartered in Panchkula, Haryana. We operate under Reserve Bank of India guidelines and follow strict AML, KYC, FEMA, and compliance standards.

We combine licensed financial infrastructure with modern automation to remove friction from traditionally complex processes like:
	•	Buying and selling foreign currency
	•	Sending money abroad
	•	Issuing and managing forex cards
	•	Travel insurance facilitation
	•	Education loan assistance

By integrating digital verification, automated documentation, and partner banking networks, we enable customers to complete transactions online with clarity and confidence.

Regulatory & Compliance

RBP FINIVIS operates as an RBI-authorized Full-Fledged Money Changer (FFMC).
We follow strict internal controls for:
	•	Customer verification (KYC)
	•	Anti-Money Laundering (AML)
	•	Counter Terrorist Financing (CTF)
	•	FEMA and LRS compliance

We act as a facilitator where applicable, and all regulated financial transactions are executed through authorized banking and financial partners.
